What is Vastu

Vastu (vāstu śāstra) is a system of architecture which literally translates to "science of architecture”. Ancient texts on Vastu elaborate in detail the principles of design, layout, measure- ments, ground preparation, space arrangement and spatial geometry, to give prosperity and happiness to the occupants of a given space. Historians like James Fergusson and Dr. Havell suggest that Vastu Shastra developed between 6000 BCE and 3000 BCE.
